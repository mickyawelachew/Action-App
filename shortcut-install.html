iOS Shortcut Installation

1 Copy the App URL

First, copy the app URL:
https://action-app.vercel.app/

2 Open the Shortcuts App

Click the button below to open our custom installation shortcut:

Open Shortcut
3 Add the Shortcut

Tap "Add Shortcut" when prompted

4 Paste the URL

When the shortcut runs, paste the URL you copied in step 1.

5 Complete Setup

The shortcut will automatically create an icon on your home screen with the Action App logo.

Need help? If you encounter any issues, try the standard installation method by opening Safari, navigating to our app, and selecting "Add to Home Screen" from the share menu.
